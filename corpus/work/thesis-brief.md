# Capstone brief — "Wayfinding for transit anxiety"

Goal: a working interactive prototype that helps anxious transit riders get through disruptions calmly.

Scope:
- Core flow: pick a route → a calm, low-information "what to do now" screen. (prototyped)
- "Live disruption" screen: when a line goes down, walk the rider to a fallback without overload. (not built)

Deliverable: a working interactive prototype plus a short written reflection.
Assessment: advisor sign-off (Mara Okafor), then final submission to NAD.
Submission deadline: 2026-06-19 (NAD term schedule).
